Brijesh Chinai

Brijesh Chinai's interests span business, education, healthcare, agriculture, design, automobiles and India's cultural legacy. While these fields may appear diverse, they are united by a common belief that enduring societies are built through strong institutions, thoughtful leadership and a deep respect for craftsmanship, history and innovation. As a trained architect, Brijesh approaches every initiative with a long-term perspective, believing that meaningful enterprises should create lasting value for people, communities and future generations.

A sixth-generation member of a family with over 150 years of entrepreneurial history, Brijesh comes from a legacy rooted in enterprise, public service, and institution-building. The Chinai family played an active role in India's freedom movement and later contributed to nation-building through business, public life, and institutions including the Indian Merchants' Chamber (IMC) and FICCI. Together with his wife, Chamundeshwari Bhogilal, Brijesh also carries forward the vision of the late Pranlal Bhogilal — an Industrialist, philanthropist, and art collector, whose passion for vintage and classic automobiles and preservation laid the foundation for Dastan Autoworld — the world-renowned Auto collection.

With a keen interest in conservation and the preservation of historic places and collections, Brijesh remains actively involved in initiatives that celebrate India's cultural, architectural, and automotive legacy. His work and areas of impact extend across real estate solutions, higher education, healthcare, agriculture and philanthropy. He also serves on the board of the century-old Chinai Hospital in Ahmedabad, an institution founded by Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, reflecting his continued commitment to strengthening institutions that create enduring value for society.
