Brijesh Chinai

Founder & Chairperson · BRIC Group of Companies

Brijesh Chinai's work spans business, education, healthcare, agriculture, heritage and conservation. Across these seemingly different fields is one common belief: strong societies are built by strong institutions, responsible leadership and a commitment to preserving what matters. As Founder and Chairperson of the BRIC Group of Companies, he approaches every initiative with a long-term perspective, believing that business should create value not only for the present, but for generations to come.

A sixth-generation member of a family with over 150 years of entrepreneurial history, Brijesh comes from a legacy rooted in enterprise, public service and institution building. The Chinai family played an active role in India's freedom movement and later contributed to nation building through business, public life and institutions including the Indian Merchants' Chamber (IMC) and FICCI. Through his marriage to Chamundeshwari Bhogilal, he is also connected to the legacy of the late Pranlal Bhogilal, one of India's most respected industrialists and collectors, whose lifelong commitment to heritage preservation continues through Dastan and Auto World.

Today, Brijesh remains actively involved across higher education, healthcare, agriculture, architecture and heritage conservation. He serves on the board of the century-old Chinai Hospital, founded by Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, and continues to support institutions and initiatives that preserve India's cultural heritage while contributing to stronger communities and meaningful long-term development.
